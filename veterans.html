Education Benefits

  • GI Bill® Basics: Confirm eligibility, apply, and monitor remaining entitlement.
  • Certification Tips: Register early, verify classes match your degree plan, and watch email for cert updates.
  • Payments: Track MHA/tuition timing; take one in-person class if required for your benefit level.

Academic Support

  • Tutoring & Writing: Use campus learning centers and online writing help.
  • Accessibility & Accommodations: Coordinate with your accessibility office early each term.
  • Time Management: Keep recurring blocks for coursework; use simple checklists and weekly review.

Community & Wellness

  • Student Veteran Groups: Join meetups, events, and service projects.
  • Recreation: Outdoor hikes, art classes, and low-cost fitness options.
  • Crisis Support: If you need help now, call or text your local emergency number or a trusted hotline.
